Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website, StingStock.

1. Data Controller

The personal data collected through this website is controlled by Lukasz Sudol (contact@lsudo.com), the operator of StingStock. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name and email address when you sign up for a newsletter or create an account.

3. How We Use and Share Information

We use the information we collect to operate our service and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal data.

To facilitate our email communications, we share your email address with a single third-party service provider:

  • SendGrid (a Twilio service): We use SendGrid to send transactional emails and newsletters. Only the information necessary to send these emails (such as your email address) is provided to them. SendGrid is prohibited from using your information for any other purpose. You can review their privacy policy for more details on how they handle data.

Other than our engagement with SendGrid for email delivery, your personal information is not shared with any other third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data. Please contact us to make such a request.
